Webinar: 90 days to TRANSFORM a ‘stuck’ digital product that HAS to succeed....

Learn the techniques four companies used to revitalise mission critical products in 90 days


Date: Monday 26th January 2015
Time: 16:00 (UK time)

As part of the Agile Product Owner Expert series, this one-off webinar is for:
• Product owners, project leaders and lead developers of...
• digital products or teams with a business critical revenue stream or service which is...
• not progressing fast enough or is ‘stuck’

We’ll share a toolkit for jumpstarting ‘stuck’ products, using examples from four inspiring product stories:
• Contiki
• Plymouth University
• Pearson PLC
• Government Digital Services (GDS)

Join us if you have a product, or products, which are:
• Critical to the success of your organisation
• Maturing fast and needing clarity and direction to stay ahead
• Under pressure from competitors
• No longer fit for purpose and losing ground
• Struggling to reinvent itself
• Loved by its internal stakeholders who are unwilling to reinvent or cannibalise it to stay in the game
• Part of a larger portfolio of products competing for airtime and resources
