15th July 2015

Masterclass: Deliver 50% growth with Lean UX

Find up to 50% more revenue from your website or 50% more conversion from your digital service during this 3-hour masterclass


Join Unboxed on Wednesday 12th August for a hands on 3-hour session where you will try out the powerful analytics-led design techniques Unboxed used to help:
• Online travel company, Contiki Holidays, increase bookings by 47%
• Pioneering NHS digital sexual health service, SH:24, increase the number of people completing the order process to 95%

Date: Wednesday 12th August
Time : 9.30am – 12.30pm (coffee and warm up fun from 9.00am)
Location: Adam House, 7-10 Adam Street, London, WC2N 6AA

The experts:

Richard Stobart – CEO at Unboxed Consulting
 Agile Coach of the Year 2014, Richard is one of the UK’s leading experts in the process of digital product design for valuable, commercial features.

Dominic Mason – Client Partner at Unboxed Consulting
 Dom works with dozens of companies each year to design projects that deliver huge value, quickly. He speaks business, technical, Agile and project.

The event in detail

This masterclass is best for optimising strategic products with a long lifespan, for example:
• Website and digital services with at least a two-year lifecycle
• eCommerce websites
• Public sector digital services
• Membership sites
• Group intranets

Who should attend?

• Those tasked with significantly increasing the revenue, conversion or performance of a website or digital service
• People who have inherited a website or digital service and need fresh ways to increase its performance
• ..or who have worked 'in the trenches' on same website or digital service for a long time

Expect to learn how to:

• Learn a methodology for creating new ‘greenfield’ Key Performance Indicator improvements
• Step up a level to the strategic product view – create more value
• Go beyond organic: Find new and unexpected sources of uplift in this metric
• Understand what drives your metrics and how to use ‘smart KPI branching’ to discover how users really behave
• Get more out of a mature product which is in diminishing returns
• Make a concrete plan for delivering the commercial return the business demands from the website or digital service
