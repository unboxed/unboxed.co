23rd February 2015

Event: Unstick your digital products: Rapidly progress a complex product or portfolio of stalled products

Date: Wednesday 25th March
Time: 9.00am – 12.30pm (coffee and warm up fun from 8.30am!)
Location: Skills Matter, 116-120 Goswell Road, London, EC1V 7DP

Join us on 25th March for an energetic three-hour session where you’ll learn a series of techniques to:

• Accelerate a product that’s buried under features
• Get a large product portfolio moving again
• Move faster to catch up with a competitor
• Get traction in a non-Agile organisation
• Focus limited resources on what will create most value

Our speakers include:

“Trust me, I’m a product manager”: case study from publishing
Dave Evans - Product Manager (E-commerce) at Nature Publishing Group

The foundation of speed is trust. Dave will talk through how to establish trust rapidly with stakeholders, how to demonstrate value and be seen as the expert.

How designing a new experience in the NHS helped to unblock and unleash new potential
Glyn Parry – Programme Manager at SH:24 (NHS collaboration)

Glyn Parry explains why an iterative and agile approach has been used to turn STI testing on its head - challenging deep seated practices and prejudices in the NHS and local authorities.

Techniques for overcoming the top digital product blockers
Richard Stobart – CEO, Unboxed Consulting, Agile Coach of the Year 2014

Exactly why digital products get stuck and techniques to speed them up again.
